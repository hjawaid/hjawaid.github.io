The Problem

Nowadays, people are constantly looking to hold on to memories by taking photos or recording videos. This is even more so the case between close family and friends. But what do we do when our phones are occupied on something like a video call?

Screen recording is an option, but requires so much effort. Not to mention spontaneous moments happen all the time during video calls, and there’s no way for us to go back in time and hit that record button.

How Might Facebook Intervene?

That’s where Facebook can jump in and use AI to help capture those key moments during video calls and solve a very real problem for families who often rely on online communication methods like video calling to keep in touch with each other.

Moments like seeing your nephew walk for the first time, your cousin announcing her engagement, or even little moments like laughing along with your family.
