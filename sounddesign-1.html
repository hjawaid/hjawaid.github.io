I was given the opportunity by Google to work on the sound design for an interactive VR mural that was based around a museum set in the future, where humans are the attraction and robots are the ongoers. However, any information discovered about humans came directly from internet culture.

I worked with an animator to create a friendly yet futuristic environment and engineer to implement it.
